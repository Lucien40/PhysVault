$$
z^{2} \dv[2]{w}{z}+z \dv{w}{z}+\pqty{z^2-v^2} w=0 .
$$
This differential equation has a regular singularity at $z=0$ with indices $\pm v$, and an irregular singularity at $z=\infty$ of rank 1.
